Project Manager

Job Summary:

Kivnon, LLC’s Project Manager will be responsible for leading and managing orders through the process of the quotation stage up to after sales. This process includes programming, installation/coordinating, training, and frequent contact with the customer on site. The Project Manager will implement each logistic automation project successfully with the customer. This role is the link between the customer and the internal organization.



The Project Manager performs a variety of duties, including, but not limited to:

• Frequent and direct contact with the client
• Main contact between the customer and the internal organization
• Gathering the customer’s specifications
• Analyzing the customer’s application in order to understand how Kivnon’s products or customizations of the product will meet the customers need
• Planning, executing, monitoring and closing projects
• Carrying out the billing control
• All other duties as assigned



Qualifications & Abilities:

• Engineering or Technical Degree preferred
• 2+ years of experience as a Project Manager in the industrial sector
• PMP Certification preferred
• Experience with PLC ́s a plus
• Experience working in logistics/automotive/AGV industry highly preferred, but not required
• Confident and assertive nature with the ability to work independently
• Strong communication, analytical, summarizing, logistic and planning skills
• High attention to detail and accuracy
• Positive attitude and ability to adapt to changing circumstances
• Ability to travel 50%



Work Environment & Physical Requirements:

• Primary work area is a controlled office environment, requiring the ability to sit for extended periods of time.
• Customer facilities typically are large manufacturing or distribution plants, requiring walking for extended periods of time



Classification & Compensation

• The position is full-time, permanent and exempt
• The pay range varies, depending on experience, skills, abilities, and length of service with the Company
